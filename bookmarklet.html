How to install:

  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B)
  2. Drag the green button above to your bookmarks bar
  3. Done

How to use:

  1. Make sure the scraper app is running
  2. Open the ShotMarker page (192.168.100.1) in Chrome
  3. Click "SM Squadding" in your bookmarks bar
  4. Click "Push Squadding" in the scraper app
